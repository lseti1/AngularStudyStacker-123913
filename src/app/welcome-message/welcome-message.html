Welcome to Study Stacker

Create your own flashcard decks and learn at your own pace.

Features include:

  • Add, edit, and delete decks & flashcards
  • Auto-flipping cards with a custom timer
  • Set learning goals per session
  • and more!

Note: This is a demo application. Data is saved in your browser's local storage and can be cleared anytime via the settings. Not all features may work as intended, and this is not a full-fledged learning platform. Best Viewed in Full Screen on a PC.
